“To love is an act of courage.” — Søren Kierkegaard

Love is not a feeling that arrives when everything is calm; it is a force that reveals who we are when everything falls apart.

To love is to allow someone to see our cracks without fearing their departure, and still remain steady, because the truth of love is not found in perfection but in surrender.

Whoever truly loves does not dominate nor possess; they walk beside, breathe, and allow the other to be.

And in that silent act of understanding, we discover that love is not a path outward but a return inward: to what we are, to what we may become, to the eternal that lives within each of us.
